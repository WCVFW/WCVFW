Who We Are & What We Stand For

Who We Are

The We Can Voice for Women Foundation is a grassroots NGO committed to gender equity and women’s empowerment across India. Founded in 2019, we believe empowering women is not just vital — it's a national priority.

What We Stand For

Empowerment is multi-dimensional — education, healthcare, economic independence, and legal rights. We work to dismantle barriers and build a just, inclusive future where women thrive with dignity and autonomy.

Our Vision & Mission

Empowering Women. Transforming Futures.

Vision

To create a society where women are enlightened, healthy, and empowered — capable of making informed choices, living independently, and contributing meaningfully to nation-building.

Vision Pillars

  • Enlightenment – Equal access to quality education and literacy.
  • Enhealthment – Physical and mental health access and awareness.
  • Empowerment – Economic independence via skills and entrepreneurship.

Mission

To provide holistic support systems that empower women to live with independence, dignity, and purpose. We aim to create safe, inclusive environments where women overcome barriers and become leaders in their communities.

Our Core Focus Areas

Empowering women through holistic, community-driven initiatives.

Gender Equality & Advocacy

Campaigns, awareness drives, and legal aid for women's rights.

Community Engagement

Mobilizing local communities to challenge gender norms and promote inclusive development.

Counseling & Support

Emotional and psychological support services for women in crisis.

Target Beneficiaries

  • Women and girls from marginalized and underserved communities
  • Survivors of domestic violence and gender-based discrimination
  • Unemployed or underemployed women seeking economic opportunities
  • Adolescent girls in need of health and reproductive education
  • Rural women needing literacy and skills development

Partners & Collaborations

We join hands with key institutions to drive real, lasting change.

Government Agencies

Working with public bodies to implement community-based policies and legal support.

NGOs & Global Organizations

Partnering with like-minded non-profits to expand impact nationally and internationally.

CSR & Corporate Allies

Collaborating with companies to fund and co-execute community programs.

Academic Institutions

Engaging universities and schools to build education-led empowerment.

Media & Advocacy Groups
